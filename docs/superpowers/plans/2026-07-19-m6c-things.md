**Goal:** Replace the box-car with real CC0 low-poly car models, plant traffic lights at real OSM signal positions, and ship the license/credits screen the CC0/ODbL assets require.

**Architecture:** Pipeline parses `highway=traffic_signals` nodes and bakes procedural traffic-light props into tiles (same pattern as lamps); a model-sourcing task downloads a CC0 vehicle pack (Quaternius preferred, poly.pizza fallback), commits 4–6 GLBs under `game/assets/models/cars/`, and `car_visual.gd` v2 instantiates a model variant by `color_index` with the procedural blocky car as automatic fallback. A credits overlay (C key) lists OSM/HRDEM/ambientCG/model attributions, mirrored in `CREDITS.md`.

**Tech Stack:** Python (pipeline), Godot 4.5, WebSearch/WebFetch for asset sourcing.
